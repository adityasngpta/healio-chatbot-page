Helpful Resources

Here are some trusted resources that might help when you're feeling overwhelmed or need support:

Crisis Support

If you need immediate help or are experiencing a crisis, these services provide 24/7 support:

  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741 for free 24/7 crisis counseling
  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988 any time
  • The Trevor Project: Support for LGBTQ+ youth - call 1-866-488-7386

Stress Management

Evidence-based resources for managing stress and anxiety:

  • Headspace for Students - Meditation app with student pricing
  • Calm for Schools - Free mindfulness resources
  • MindShift CBT - Free anxiety management app

Academic Support

Resources to help manage academic stress:

  • Khan Academy - Free expert-created content
  • edX - Free courses from top institutions
  • Coursera Free Courses - Various topics

Mental Health Apps

Mobile apps for mental well-being:

  • Happify - Science-based activities and games
  • Wysa - AI chatbot for emotional support
  • Daylio - Mood tracking and journaling

Self-Help Books

Recommended reading for mental health:

  • "Feeling Good: The New Mood Therapy" by David D. Burns
  • "Atomic Habits" by James Clear
  • "The Anxiety and Phobia Workbook" by Edmund J. Bourne

Important Notice

If you're experiencing a medical emergency or are in immediate danger, please call emergency services (911 in the US) right away. These resources are not substitutes for professional care.
